Getting Started


The Home Automation dashboard has multiple screens that can be selected from the left hand tabs.

Use the bottom left icons to toggle the widget toolbox and also create, delete and save screens. Hover over the icons for help with design functions.

Widgets can be moved by dragging with the mouse, re-scaled by selecting the widget and dragging the scale handles, and edited by right clicking to display the attributes menu.
